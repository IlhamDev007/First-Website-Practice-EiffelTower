Eiffel Tower Summit

☼ After you complete your tour around the second floor, it’s time to move to the top of the tower, the Eiffel Tower Summit.

☼ You will have to use a lift from the second floor to get to the summit, as the stairways are closed to visitors.

☼ These magnificent glass-walled lifts will take you 276 meters high above the ground.

☼ At this height, enjoy the mild breezes of wind while you witness the precious beauty of the city of love.
